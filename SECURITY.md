# Security Policy

Notulee runs entirely in your browser or on your computer and stores notes locally — but if you believe
you've found a security or privacy vulnerability, we want to fix it quickly and quietly.

## Please do **not** open a public issue for security problems

Instead, report it privately by **either**:

- Using GitHub's **[Report a vulnerability](https://github.com/Notulee/community/security/advisories/new)** (private advisory), **or**
- Emailing **adam@najmanowicz.com** with the subject `Notulee security`.

Please include:

- A description of the issue and its impact
- Steps to reproduce, or a proof of concept
- The browser/OS and roughly when you loaded the app

We'll acknowledge your report as soon as we can and keep you posted on the fix.
Thank you for helping keep Notulee users safe. 🔒
