# Community Guidelines

This is a small, friendly space for Notulee users. A few simple rules keep it that way.

## Do

- **Be respectful.** Assume good faith. Critique ideas, not people.
- **Stay on topic.** Keep posts about Notulee — bugs, ideas, help, tips.
- **Search first.** Someone may have already asked or reported it.
- **Help others** when you can. A good answer helps the next person too.

## Don't

- No harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or discrimination.
- No spam, advertising, or off-topic self-promotion.
- No sharing of other people's private information.
- No NSFW or illegal content.

## Moderation

Maintainers and moderators may edit, move, lock, or remove posts that break these rules,
and may suspend repeat offenders. If you see something that doesn't belong, use the
**Report** option on the post, or email **adam@najmanowicz.com**.

We want this to be a welcoming place — thanks for helping keep it that way. 💛
